To plan and journal meaningfully.


Flux doesn't aim to reinvent the wheel — just digitize a simple and thoughtful process with some bells and whistles.


You can even try it first with a pen and paper!

The Technique

Traditional To-Do

A To-Do task usually has only two outcomes:

  • ✓ Done - Mark complete
  • ✗ Not done - Leave as is
Plan & Journal
After forever, the weather is finally nice. Will bike for 30-40m.

Instead of just a checkbox, each task tells a story:

Positive Reinforcement
Got carried away and biked for an hour — love circling around Charles river!
Negative Reinforcement
Felt too lazy and just stayed in. Will try tomorrow :(
Or more!
Biked for almost two hours but ended up queasy — Need to know my limit.
